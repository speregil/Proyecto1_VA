Creating the new Dino-Packs!

There was a study made with people who visited the park. It revealed 2 facts:

  1. People complained about the entrance cost
  2. People said they get too tired during the day trying to visit all the rides and shows. They also said they prefer visit the park just for half a day during the weekend.

With the data that was gathered during the weekend that Scott Jones visited the park, the market team has 2 strategies: restrict the available rides the people has during the days and create combo packs with half days during the weekends so they can offer cheaper tickets.
